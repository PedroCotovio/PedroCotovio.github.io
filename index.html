Bio

I’m a master student in Lasige’s data and systems intelligence line at the University of Lisbon and the Intelligent Systems Group at Fraunhofer Portugal (AICOS), supervised by Professor Sara Madeira and PhD André Carreiro at the Faculty of Sciences.

My current research focuses on using deep reinforcement learning approaches to improve algorithms for n-way data analysis.

I am interested in all things deep & reinforcement learning, particularly closing the gap between artificial nets and their biological counterparts and the emergence of conscious systems.
